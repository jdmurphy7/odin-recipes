Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 jar peanut butter
  • 1 jar jelly

Steps

  1. Place the 2 slices of bread next to each other on a paper towel
  2. Spread the peanut butter on 1 of the slices of bread with a knife
  3. Spread the jelly on the other slice of bread with a spoon
  4. Stack the 2 pieces of bread together, jelly facing peanut butter.
